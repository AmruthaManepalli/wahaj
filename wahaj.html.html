WAHAJ ALI

Introduction

Wahaj was born on 1 December 1988 into a Punjabi Muslim family in Lahore, Punjab. His father worked in the government, and his mother was a teacher. The only child of his parents, Ali is a business graduate, with a specialization in finance. His parents wanted him to take the Central Superior Services (CSS) exam and join the civil service, but his inclination was towards acting. Ali then earned his Masters in Multimedia Arts from the National College of Arts. He met his wife, Sana Farooq, during an internship program on Samaa TV and married on 3rd March, 2016. The couple had a daughter in 2017, Amirah Ali.

Wahaj Ali is a Pakistani actor who works in Urdu TV industry. He made his debut with Hum TV drama i.e., Ishq Ibadat as Sahil, and since then he has appeared in several drama serials. His career is full of great achievements where he played amazing roles in many top rated dramas In 2020, Wahaj portrayed the pivotal role of Shariq Habib in Ehd-e-Wafa drama. Later in 2021, Wahaj portrayed Hamza in Fitoor and received critical praise for playing the freedom fighter Shafi Imam Rumi in the period drama Jo Bichar Gaye. As of 2023, Wahaj is gaining fame by portraying Murtasim Khan in Geo TV’s drama i.e., Tere Bin and Saad in ARY Digital’s romantic drama Mujhe Pyaar Hua Tha. Wahaj Ali is the only child in his family (no brothers or sisters). His father is government employee, and his mother in education department. Ali graduated in business (in finance). His parents wanted him to take the Central Superior Services (CSS) exam and join the civil service, but he wanted to pursue acting. He worked on the research team of Nadia Jamil’s morning show on Samaa TV. He then earned his Masters in Multimedia Arts from the National College of Arts.
